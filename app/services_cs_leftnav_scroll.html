Stock Advisor

Stock picks from our founders

Stock Advisor is an online membership to stock research that focuses on seeking out solid businesses with proven management and reliable balance sheets. Lead by Brothers David and Tom Gardner, co-founders of the Fool, Stock Advisor is for those who are looking for stock recommendations spanning a wide spectrum of volatility and investing risk alongside a buy and hold investing outlook of at least 3-5 years.

Membership includes the following:
  • - Unlimited access to the Stock Advisor member-only website on www.Fool.com
  • - 2 Stock recommendations per month – one each from Tom and David Gardner
  • - Continued coverage of all previous recommendations
  • - Sell notices, when appropriate
  • - Weekly email updates on Fridays
  • - Access to Stock Advisor Special Reports

Motley Fool Supernova

Growth stock model portfolios

The Motley Fool Supernova is a model portfolio service that members can follow alongside by executing trades themselves in their own brokerage account. To follow along, you will be able to review all positions and allocations within the portfolios at any time. Supernova’s multiple portfolios utilize growth stocks from The Motley Fool’s Co-Founder David Gardner’s Stock Advisor and Rule Breakers recommendation lists. The portfolios in Supernova include Odyssey 1 and 2 which are designed to model an investor who is looking to invest consistently for the next 5+ years and will add cash every other week to achieve the development of a robust growth portfolio. The Phoenix 1 and 2 portfolios are designed to model an investor who is looking to take an existing amount of money and invest it aggressively over the next 2.5 years to achieve a growth portfolio to be used as an income source by means of regular withdraws. Supernova is for investors who want an aggressive growth stock portfolio, can tolerate the higher than average levels of volatility and investing risk associated with this type of strategy, want follow alongside a model portfolio while executing trades themselves, and that have a portfolios of at least $25,000 (Odyssey 1,2) or $30,000 (Phoenix 1,2).

Membership includes the following:
  • - Online access to premium content on Supernova.Fool.com
  • - Timely Trade Alerts and explanations from the Supernova Portfolios (approximately 3-5 per month in each portfolio)
  • - Access to two “Exploration Missions” where our analysts choose their favorite stocks based on a theme or David Gardner’s Best Buys Now
  • - Includes an Allocation Calculator to help allocate the correct amount of funds to match your chosen portfolios
  • - Unlimited access to The Motley Fool Stock Advisor and Rule Breakers newsletters services
  • - Supernova weekly email updates from each portfolio and a recap email every Friday
  • - Access to Supernova Community Discussion Boards

Motley Fool Pro

Market hedging model portfolio

The Motley Fool Pro is a model portfolio service that members can follow alongside by executing trades themselves in their own brokerage account. To follow along, you will be able to review all positions, allocations, and strategies within the portfolio at any time. In addition to long positions, Pro uses several different options strategies, and employs ETFs, hedges, shorts, and other advanced strategies to make money in flat, down, and up markets. Pro is for investors seeking to hedge market volatility, follow alongside a model portfolio while executing trades themselves, and that have a portfolio of $200,000 or more.

Motley Fool Options

Options trade recommendations

Motley Fool Options is an online recommendation service that issues options trades on stocks. Lead Advisors Jeff Fischer and Jim Gillies and their team make options recommendations to provide a steady stream of profitable options ideas to complement members existing portfolios. This service is geared toward beginning or intermediate options investors, looking to generate income, protect profits, hedge their stock portfolio, in addition to having the ability to buy/sell stocks at better prices. Our advisors recommend having at least $50,000 in your portfolio to take full advantage of this service.

Membership includes the following:
  • - Online access to premium content on Options.Fool.com
  • - Recommended Options Trades (approximately 4 each month)
  • - Access to Options U, our online educational resource for learning and understanding different options trading strategies
  • - Options weekly email updates, Every Friday
  • - Monthly live video chats with the Options team
  • - Access to Options Community Discussion Boards

Current Trend Research
